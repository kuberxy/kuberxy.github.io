状语从句

状语从句，即一个句子，在另一个句子中作状语。

一、时间状语从句

1.1、when … & as … & while …（当…的时候）

when和as引导的从句多用一般态，可接短暂动词，也可接延续动词。而while引导的从句多用进行态，强调动作的进行，只能接延续动词。

  • When he began to play a tune, we had our first glimpse of the snake.
  • I looked down and nearly fell off the ladder when I saw a policeman.
  • As the thieves were trying to get away in their car, Roy drove his bus into then back of it.
  • As she walked away, I followed her out of the fair.
  • While he was eating, I asked him to lend me twenty pounds.
  • While the battered car was moving away, Roy stopped his bus and telephoned the police.

注：as是一个万能词，可接一般态，也可接进行态

1.1.1、when

when有时候也有”就在此时“的意思，用以说明一个分句的时间。这时，when引导的从句必须放在主句后面。

  • I had nearly reached the town, when the young man suddenly said, very slowly, ‘Do you speak English?’
  • I was almost there when a sarcastic voice below said, ‘I don’t think the windows need cleaning at this time of the night.’

如果时间状语从句表示将来，一般将来时要改为一般现在时。比如，

I will be a teacher when I grow up.

这里，虽然“长大”发生在将来，但因为是时间状语从句，所以用一般现在时。

1.1.2、whenever

表示”无论何时，每当“，是when的强调形式。

  • Whenever you have an aim, you must sacrifice something of freedom to attain it.
  • Forgive others whenever you can.

1.1.3、while

既可以作副词连词，也可以作并列连词。

在表示“当…时”的时候，while视为副词连词，引导状语从句，此时两个句子通常时态不同。

  • While two detectives were keeping guard at the door, two others opened the parcel.
  • A robot-arm will grab the telescope and hold it while the astronauts make the necessary repairs.

在表示“而…”的时候，while视为并列连词，连接两个分句，此时两个句子时态相同。

  • When the plane arrived, some of the detectives were waiting inside the main building while others were waiting on the airfield.

1.2、before … & after … （在…之前/在…之后）

通常接短暂动词，但也可接延续动词

  • After I had left a small village in the south of France, I drove on to the next town.
  • After he has retired, he will devote himself to gardening.
  • Before he retired, Frank was the head of a very large business company, but as a boy he used to work in a small shop.

before用以说明主句的时间很长时，翻译为“才”；用以说明主句的时间很短时，翻译为”就“。这时，before引导的从句必须放在主句后面。

  • Nearly a week passed before the girl was able to explain what had happened to her.

  • Only three days passed before the girl was able to explain what had happened to her.

1.3、until & till （直到…）

until或till引导的从句只能接短暂动词。当主句是肯定语气时，用延续动词；当主句是否定语气时，用短暂动词。

  • He waited until the volcano became quiet and he was able to return two days later.（not … until …翻译为“直到…才…”）

  • The young man did not wake up until the bed had struck the ground.

1.4、as soon as & the moment （一…就…）

只能接短暂动词。

  • As soon as he had got into the car, I said good morning to him in French and he replied in the same language.
  • As soon as this was done, they cooked a meal over an open fire.
  • The moment you leave this tent, you will get a big surprise.

1.5、no sooner … than … & hardly/scarcely … when/before … （刚…就…）

no sooner … than …表示主句动作已完成，hardly/scarcely … when/before …表示主句动作差点完成。通常情况下，主句用过去完成时，从句用一般过去时，并且只能接短暂动词。

  • He had no sooner returned than he bought a house and went to live there.

  • He had hardly had time to settle down when he sold the house and left the country.

  • I had scarcely fallen asleep before the noise from neighbor woke me up.

1.6、since & ever since（自从…）

可接短暂动词，也可接延续动词。

  • He has just bought a new house in the city, but ever since he moved in, he has had trouble with cars and their owners.
  • We haven’t seen each other since we graduated.

注：since后不能接完成时，通常是一般过去时

1.7、翻译练习

当我走进这座大楼的时候，看见一位拿着公文包的女士（while）。

当你长大，你就会知道父母有多伟大。

每当你发现你和多数人站在同一边时，就是改革的时候了。

玛丽打开礼物后，便开始写感谢函（thank-you notes）了。

雨一停，天空中就出现了一道彩虹。

比赛才刚刚开始，两个队就打了起来。（no sooner … than）

Lulu刚用她的新电脑，电脑就坏了。（hardly … when）

我们会待在海边直到天黑。

已经过了三个月，大卫才提到他生病了。

我在电影院门口等Lisa，不久以后她就到了。
